整理範圍
2026 HIV drug-focused literature：ART、PrEP、named antiretroviral agents / regimens。
規則
Search
PubMed 2026 records containing HIV plus ART / PrEP / named antiretroviral drug or regimen terms.
Exclusion
Excluded records primarily about HCV, HPV, cancer, mental health, QoL, nutrition, oral health, vaccination, cost, surveillance, STI-only topics, or other low drug-relevance themes.
Inclusion
Included records with named HIV drugs or clinically relevant drug-decision signals, including efficacy, safety, resistance, switch, virologic suppression/failure, pharmacokinetics, clinical trial, systematic review, or guideline.
Priority
High priority includes RCT/clinical trial/meta-analysis/systematic review/guideline, or high-interest agents such as lenacapavir, CAB/RPV, or doravirine/islatravir in core treatment/prevention topics. Medium and Low reflect decreasing immediate clinical actionability.
Asia priority adjustment
If title or abstract explicitly identifies Asian study data, priority is promoted by one level and Asian records are sorted earlier within the same priority tier. Author-affiliation-only inferred locations are not used for this boost.
